I am a professor and current department chair in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Notre Dame in South Bend, Indiana. I am a member of the Discrete Math, Operations Research, and Probability group. Please go here to see a short version of my CV, here to see my full CV, and here to track my mathematical tour of the US.

My research interests are in combinatorics, graph theory and discrete probability. My research is (and has been) supported by the Simons Foundation's Division for Mathematics and the Physical Sciences, and has in the past been supported by the National Security Agency's Mathematical Sciences Program, and by American Institute of Mathematics (via a SQuaRE); I'm very grateful to all three organizations!
